Pasta Salad

Description

Pasta salad is a versatile and satisfying dish that is perfect for picnics, barbecues, or as a refreshing side dish. This recipe will guide you through creating a delightful pasta salad filled with vibrant vegetables, flavorful dressing, and al dente pasta. The combination of textures and flavors in this salad is sure to impress your guests or make for a delicious meal any time of the year. Customize it with your favorite vegetables and herbs for a personalized touch. Enjoy this refreshing and tasty pasta salad!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces rotini or fusilli pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup bell pepper, diced (any color you prefer)
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup black olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
For the Dressing:
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and rinse under cold water to stop the cooking process. Set aside.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, red onion, black olives, feta cheese, and fresh basil. Toss to mix the vegetables evenly.
  3. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, minced garlic, Dijon mustard, dried oregano, salt, and pepper to create the dressing.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the vegetable mixture and pour the dressing over the top.
  5. Gently toss all the ingredients together until the pasta and vegetables are well coated with the dressing.
  6. Refrigerate the pasta salad for at least 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld together.
  7. Before serving, give the pasta salad a quick toss and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  8. Serve chilled and enjoy this refreshing and flavorful pasta salad!
